Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for the HadsaMed App

At HadsaMed, your privacy and data security are a top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you use our mobile application ("App") and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information

  • Full name, email address, phone number, address
  • Date of birth and gender
  • Medical history and health-related data you provide

b. Usage Information

  • Log data (e.g., time and duration of visits)
  • Device information (model, operating system, etc.)
  • App interaction and service usage patterns

c. Payment Information

  • Transaction data processed securely through our payment providers (Note: We do not store your credit/debit card details directly.)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize healthcare services
  • Connect you with doctors, nurses, pharmacists, and healthcare providers
  • Send appointment reminders and service updates
  • Improve the app's performance and user experience
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it with:

  • Licensed healthcare professionals affiliated with HadsaMed
  • Service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., IT, hosting)
  • Authorities, when required by law

All parties we share information with are obligated to protect your data.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • End-to-end encryption
  • Secure servers and data storage
  • Role-based access control to sensitive medical data

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Ask questions about how your data is used

To exercise these rights, contact us at: info@hadsamed.com

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal and medical data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You'll be notified of major changes through the app or via email.
